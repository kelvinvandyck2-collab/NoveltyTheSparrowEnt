✨ KEY FEATURES HIGHLIGHT

FIFO/FEFO System:
  • Oldest expiry date sells first automatically
  • GET /api/batches/next/:barcode returns FEFO batch
  • Prevents expired product sales

Unit Conversion:
  • Automatic conversion from packaging units to selling units
  • 10 boxes × 24 bottles/box = 240 units
  • Handled transparently in all operations

Audit Trail:
  • Every transaction logged in inventory_audit_log
  • User ID, timestamp, branch, action type, reference
  • Complete traceability for compliance

Multi-Branch Support:
  • Transfer stock between branches at cost price
  • Branch-specific pricing possible
  • Per-branch reorder alerts
  • Branch filtering in all reports

Real-time Alerts:
  • Critical: Stock = 0
  • High: Stock ≤ Reorder Level
  • Normal: Stock > Reorder Level
  • Suggested quantity = Reorder Level × 2

Auto-Markup Calculation:
  • Cost Price × (1 + Markup%) = Selling Price
  • Example: ₵8.00 × 1.05 = ₵8.40
